Paratha

Description

A warm, freshly baked flatbread to accompany any of the other recipes!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup wheat flour
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 2 teaspoons oil
  • 1/2 cup water
  • Salt
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Mix flour, cumin seeds, oil, and salt in a medium size bowl
  2. Add 1/2 cup water and knead the dough
  3. Divide into 6 equal parts and give each part a round shape
  4. Roll out dough patties on roti making board using a rolling pin
  5. Spread a few drops of oil on the dough and coat with dry flour
  6. Place it on a hot griddle and cook over medium flame
  7. Flip and cook on the other side
  8. Spread butter as per your liking
